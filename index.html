About Us

Shanti Senior Secondary School is committed to providing a balanced education that focuses on academic excellence, character development, and holistic growth. Our mission is to nurture confident, responsible citizens.

Our Programs

We offer a wide range of programs to cater to the diverse needs and interests of our students, from academics to extracurricular activities.

Science Stream

Our science stream is designed to foster analytical thinking and problem-solving skills, preparing students for careers in medicine, engineering, and research.

Commerce Stream

The commerce stream offers a strong foundation in business, finance, and economics, equipping students with the skills needed for success in the corporate world.

Arts Stream

Our arts stream encourages creativity, critical thinking, and cultural awareness, preparing students for careers in the humanities, social sciences, and more.

School Facilities

Our school offers state-of-the-art facilities to support our students' academic and extracurricular pursuits, ensuring a well-rounded education.

Modern Classrooms

Our classrooms are equipped with the latest technology to enhance the learning experience.

Science Labs

Our well-equipped labs provide students with hands-on experience in scientific exploration.

Library

Our extensive library resources support both academic research and recreational reading.

Sports Facilities

Our sports facilities encourage physical fitness and teamwork through a variety of activities.
